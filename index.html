WHO WE ARE

ClimateProof enables companies and individuals to leave their individual footprint towards achieving the Paris Climate Agreement.


The first of its kind ClimateProof platform enables companies and all parts of society to reduce their environmental impact by digitalizing verification processes, tracing and trading CO2 reduction and including environmental aspects in their decision taking process.
